What's On
Grand City has held several big events from travel fair to culinary festival in order to provide meaningful experience for customers. Some of our events are Summer Kids Holiday, Java Jazz On The Move, Hosgeldin Ramazan, Jakarta Pet Expo with Social Dog, and many more.


SUMMER KIDS HOLIDAY

It’s coming real soon on this summer! Get ready to feel the joy where you can have fun and play at Grand City.



JAVA JAZZ ON THE MOVE

The Jakarta International Java Jazz Festival will be back in 2022 to bring hope and inspiration for the industry and everyone involved within. Music will uplift the spirit of its lovers.



HOSGELDIN RAMAZAN

Carrying the Turkey Vibes concept, Hosgeldin Ramazan presents a variety of interesting activities, such as the Ramadhan Thrifting Market.



JAKARTA PET EXPO WITH SOCIAL DOG

Various activities such as Dog Community Gathering, Animal Communication, Dog Eating Contest, Dog Costume Contest for Dog, and Exotic Pet Lovers will be held at Grand City.
